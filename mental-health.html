Simple Self-Care Tips

Small actions, big impact. Start with just one today.

Prioritize Sleep

Try to get 7–8 hours of sleep. Put your phone away 30 minutes before bed. Your brain needs rest to function well.

Move Your Body

Even a 15-minute walk can boost your mood. Exercise releases endorphins — your brain's natural feel-good chemicals.

Talk to Someone

You don't have to figure everything out alone. Reach out to a friend, family member, or a helpline. Talking helps.

Take Real Breaks

Scrolling social media isn't a break. Step away from screens. Drink water. Stretch. Give your mind actual downtime.
