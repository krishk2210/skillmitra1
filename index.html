The Problems We're Solving

Millions of students in India face three critical challenges that hold them back.

01

The Skill Gap

College degrees don't teach practical, job-ready skills. Students graduate without knowing how to market, design, code, or write professionally.

02

No Income Source

Most students depend on family for money. They want to earn, but lack the skills, networks, and platforms to find legitimate freelance work.

03

Mental Stress

Academic pressure, financial anxiety, and career uncertainty take a heavy toll. Mental health support is scarce, expensive, and stigmatized.

One Platform. Three Solutions.

SkillMitra tackles all three challenges in a single, free, easy-to-use platform designed specifically for Indian students.

Free Skill Courses

Practical, industry-relevant courses in digital marketing, design, tech, and writing — all free for students.

Real Freelance Jobs

A curated job board with verified clients, budget transparency, and beginner-friendly opportunities.

Mental Wellness Tools

Breathing exercises, daily check-ins, anonymous peer support, and helpline resources — all free and private.

Simple Self-Care Tips

Try to get 7–8 hours. Put your phone away 30 minutes before bed.

Move Your Body
